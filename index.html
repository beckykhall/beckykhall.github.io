About Me

I came into my web development career a little sideways, starting with an art degree and a photography business, but I couldn’t silence the siren call of good coding practices for long. Projects

Cart Page Samples Feature

In order to reduce the friction of the checkout process, my team designed an A/B test in which I replaced a disruptive, slow-loading samples popup with a responsive samples section that appears beneath the item summary on the cart page. In a later iteration, I also added support for configurable samples.

Find Similar Products Feature

To improve the experience of users running into out of stock products, my team designed a “Find Similar” feature that would allow users to find similar products when confronted with out of stock options. I built out a link in Maxymiser based on the most specific category for the current product, as well as any color, size, or width options in which the user had shown interest.

Accordion Video Slider Feature

Product videos were proving to be a successful addition to this cosmetics company website, so my team designed an A/B test to increase views, in which I moved videos from the thumbnails section of the product image into their own section at the bottom of the product details accordion and opened that section by default. If a product had more than one video, I contained them all within a responsive slider.
